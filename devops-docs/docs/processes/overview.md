# DevOps Processes

This section outlines all standard DevOps processes and workflows within our organization.

## Available Processes

### [Release Management](release-management.md)
The complete process for managing software releases from development through production.

### [Change Management](change-management.md)
Procedures for making safe and coordinated changes to production systems.

### [Incident Response](incident-response.md)
Guidelines for responding to and resolving production incidents.

### [Deployment Workflow](deployment-workflow.md)
Step-by-step procedures for deploying applications across different environments.

## General Guidelines

All processes must be followed to ensure:
- System stability and reliability
- Proper documentation and tracking
- Team coordination and communication
- Compliance with organizational standards

For questions about any process, contact the DevOps team lead.
